# Alternatives & Comparisons

> Looking for a way to give your AI agent access to your Mac? Here's how Clawsy compares to common approaches.

## vs. Manual copy-paste
Copy-pasting context into your AI chat manually works but doesn't scale. Clawsy automates clipboard sync, screenshots, and file access so your agent always has context without interrupting your workflow.

## vs. Screen sharing / remote desktop
Screen sharing tools like VNC or RDP require a human on both ends. Clawsy is designed for unattended agent access — your agent can request a screenshot at any time, even when you're not actively watching.

## vs. AppleScript / Automator
AppleScript can automate Mac tasks but requires significant setup and doesn't integrate with AI agent sessions. Clawsy provides a ready-made bridge with permission dialogs, SSH tunneling, and session routing built in.

## vs. Building it yourself
You could build a custom WebSocket bridge between your Mac and your agent. Clawsy is that bridge — already built, open source, and MIT licensed. Fork it if you need custom behavior.
